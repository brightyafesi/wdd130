THEMES
Let us introduce you to some of the themes among the concerns in the world which require a good touch from the film industry.

Insecurity:
Insecurity eventually ends down to the individual because law enforcements would not be everywhere all the time.
Mediocre self-defence skills which are now becoming more common are a risk to the practitioners if faced with danger.
Providing authentic survival skills and regularly improving them reduces the chances of insecurity.
And screening the people receiving the training to prevent the wrong people who rather than protecting themselves, they may instead victimise others.
Neglecting military personnel in elite units after their service results to them transferring their skills to elaborate crime. Catering for these people with something like veteran community support programs would be beneficial.

High Population:
The main concern for high population is scarcity of resources.
High population is more advantageous than disadvantageous, If the global powers (big governments/ big companies) are less interested in ruling over or controlling the world and more interested in managing it.
With environmental conservation and good recycling systems, resources would not be scarce however much the population grows. The Earth was created to support life in all its diversity. The more the people the greater the creativity and diversity of humanity.

Machine Labour:
The major concern here is that machines will replace humans at work.
Machine labour should solve human problems but not make abnormal profits.
Machines should replace humans with risky tasks at work like working in mines, harsh environments.
And humans should be allocated to the rest of all the less risky tasks at work.

Artificial Intelligence:
Computers will rule the world in the future is one of the speculations that is major concern. But if taken seriously, Artificial Intelligence is as dangerous as the programmers behind them. It is the programmers with ambitions to rule the world that attempt to build mailicious programs. So the concern should not be on computers but on the programmers. Have an environment for ethical programming then you will have ethical artificial intelligence.

CONTENT

Our themes from the home page could be protrayed in a variety of good story telling narratives
Here we present to you one such narrative for each theme from the homepage. And these are log lines which are snippets to give sample content of what the plots of bigger structured screenplays would be focused on.

Insecurity:
The insecurity theme could be made under the action film genre.
Log line:
Character X is a fitness and martial arts expert who after experiencing a series of falling a victim of mediocre self-defence from average standard schools, brought more harm than good in big threat scenarios. And made multiple failed attempts of finding a martial arts school which counter the big realistic threats out there in the world. Through circumstances of the hustling experience, Character X achieved the desired martial arts skills and as an expert strives to make these skills easily accessible to those who are only exposed to the mediocre self defence skills. Unlike the popular hero that saves the day, character X not only saves those in danger but also shows them to save themselves out of danger.

High Population:
The high population theme could be made under the political business drama film genre
Log line:
Character X is a successful down to earth entreprenuer pursuing politics without political games. Having endured the low living standards from the conjested slums facing numerous evictions, in the name of urbanisation, industralisation or modern development. To overcome the political and wealthy world stigma which designates the low class, as useless consumers populating and filling the world, character x emerges a business campaign of transforming the surbubs, slums, ghettos, wastelands from pollution, into recycling hubs and green energy residential parks for the low class. The business is then a threat and target lobbied against by the wealthy. So character x joins politics to introduce this policy for the benefit of the low class themselves, and as relief from the raising fear of high population (mostly of the poor or low class) and world pollution (mostly by the big companies).

Machine labour:
The machine labour theme could be made under the science fiction business drama film genre.
Log line:
Character X is an engineer seeking support for developing robotic equipment for risky work. After successful working for a company that grew to manufacture robotic equipment for industrial and commercial use, these machines gradually ended replacing the people's routine jobs and many lost their jobs while others would find it difficult to get a routine job at the big companies and this had a big negative impact on the society. Although sought by enterprenuers, character x is commited to design equipment for domestic use, and as for industrial and commercial use, the machines are to function as assistance to the workers, and not a replacement. After receiving commissions by the mining companies which are facing criticism for the fatal working conditions of their workers, character x embarks on developing robotic equipment for assisting the workers in the mines and reduce the casualities. And so character x sets this as a stepping stone to encourage the other engineers and business people in the rest of the industrial and commercial sector, to remember that the philanthropy from their successes is counter intuitive if it does not start from the consideration of the workers in their companies.

Artificial intelligence:
The Artificial Intelligence theme could be made under the science fiction crime film genre.
Log line:
Character X is a computer scientist seeking to have computer hackers pursue a positive purpose for their computer skills. When working for a successful software company that develops autonomous computer systems, the company was a target from spyware entities. In addition to other computer companies, spyware entities work to infiltrate their products and be able to have discreet large scale surveillance and espionage and sell the data to third parties. Character x finds that with the advancement in the Artificial Intelligence industry, when tampered with these discreet third parties, you would have control over the world systems, with a mindless, souless and spiritless entity. Since many of the hackers used these schemes are simply young talents proud of their skills, character x seeks support from the cyber division of law enforcements to recruit instead of jailing the hackers tracked down, and also attract amd motivate the hackers out there that positive computer skills are more ingenious than the negative ones. Character x, shows that if these young talents are not utilised by the government, then there skills are not safe out there in the world.
